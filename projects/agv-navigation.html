AGV Vision-Based Navigation

This project focuses on developing an autonomous AGV navigation system by integrating Artificial Potential Field (APF) and Rapidly-exploring Random Tree (RRT) with computer vision for obstacle perception.

My Role

Team Leader & Algorithm Developer

Technologies

Python, OpenCV, APF, RRT

Outcome

The system demonstrated improved navigation stability and obstacle avoidance in structured indoor environments.
